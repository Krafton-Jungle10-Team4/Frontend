# Equity Analyst Notes & Sector Outlooks (2025 H2)

## 1. Tech Sector Update: AI Hardware Sustainment
**Ticker:** NVDA, AMD, INTC
**Rating:** Overweight
**Date:** 2025-11-10
**Note:**
Demand for data center GPUs remains robust, extending the semiconductor upcycle. While hyperscaler capex growth is normalizing, enterprise adoption of on-prem AI inference is the next growth driver.

**Valuation:**
- Sector forward P/E is elevated at 28x, but justified by 20% YoY earnings growth.
- **Top Pick:** NVDA (Target: $180) - Dominant market share in inference chips remains unchallenged.

---

## 2. Consumer Discretionary: Retail Resilience
**Ticker:** AMZN, WMT, TGT
**Rating:** Neutral
**Date:** 2025-10-25
**Note:**
Consumer spending is shifting from goods to services, but holiday season forecasts are optimistic (up 4% YoY). E-commerce margins are improving due to automation in logistics.

**Risks:**
- Credit card delinquency rates rising among lower-income demographics.
- Inventory bloat in apparel sub-sector.

---

## 3. Healthcare: Biotech M&A Wave
**Sector:** Biotechnology / Pharma
**Rating:** Overweight
**Date:** 2025-11-05
**Analysis:**
With interest rates stabilizing, pharma giants are deploying cash reserves for acquisitions. Key therapeutic areas: Obesity (GLP-1 successors) and Alzheimer's treatments.

**Watchlist:**
- Small-cap biotechs with Phase 3 data due in Q4.
- Regulatory scrutiny on drug pricing (IRA implementation impacts).

---

## 4. Financials: Regional Banks Recovery
**Sector:** Banking
**Rating:** Underweight -> Neutral
**Date:** 2025-10-12
**Note:**
Regional banks have stabilized balance sheets. Deposit flight has reversed. Net Interest Margins (NIM) are compressing slightly but fee-based income is recovering. Regulatory capital requirements (Basel III endgame) remain a headwind for larger institutions.

**Top Pick:** JPM (Best-in-class fortress balance sheet).
