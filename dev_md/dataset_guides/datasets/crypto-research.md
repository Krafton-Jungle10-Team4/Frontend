# Crypto Market & On-Chain Research (2025 Q4)

## 1. Bitcoin Market Cycle Analysis
**Asset:** BTC
**Date:** 2025-11-15
**Trend:** Accumulation
**Insight:**
Post-halving supply shock is fully realized. Long-term holder (LTH) supply has reached an all-time high of 76%. Exchange reserves are at multi-year lows. Institutional ETF inflows have stabilized at ~$500M weekly avg.

**On-Chain Metrics:**
- **MVRV Z-Score:** 1.8 (Neutral/Bullish)
- **Hash Rate:** New ATH, signaling miner confidence despite revenue compression.

## 2. Ethereum L2 Ecosystem & Blob Fees
**Asset:** ETH, OP, ARB
**Date:** 2025-10-30
**Topic:** Scalability / Dencun Upgrade Follow-up
**Analysis:**
Layer 2 transaction costs have dropped 90% YoY. Total Value Locked (TVL) in L2s has flipped Ethereum Mainnet for the first time. The "Blob" fee market is active but remains cheap, suppressing ETH burn rate.

**Risks:**
- Fragmented liquidity across optimistic and ZK rollups.
- Centralization concerns in sequencer operations.

## 3. DeFi Yields & Real World Assets (RWA)
**Sector:** DeFi / RWA
**Date:** 2025-11-08
**Trend:** Institutional Integration
**Note:**
Tokenized Treasury bills (market cap $2B+) are becoming the standard collateral in DeFi lending protocols. MakerDAO and Aave leading the charge. Yields on stablecoins are averaging 5-6%, competitive with TradFi money markets.

**Watchlist:**
- Regulatory clarity on stablecoin issuance in the EU (MiCA full implementation).
- Tokenized private credit protocols.

## 4. Solana vs. Modular Blockchains
**Asset:** SOL, TIA
**Date:** 2025-10-18
**Topic:** Monolithic vs. Modular
**Insight:**
Solana's "Firedancer" validator client testnet success has boosted sentiment for monolithic scaling. Meanwhile, modular ecosystems (Celestia, Cosmos) are seeing developer traction but struggle with user experience fragmentation.

**Metric:**
- **SOL Daily Active Addresses:** +20% MoM driven by DePIN projects.
